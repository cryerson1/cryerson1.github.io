Nike Zoom Next% Features

Lightweight design

Incredibly soft yet highly responsive ride

Snappy responsiveness from carbon fiber plate

Recognized as the shoe of choice for many of the world's top athletes, the ZoomX Vaporfly Next% 2 combines an industry-leading ZoomX midsole with a full-length carbon fiber plate to create one of the fastest racing shoes to date. The Vaporfly offers a highly cushioned running experience that is both incredibly lightweight and responsive; this allows the shoe to maximize running efficiency, reduce recovery times, and propel runners to fast race-day performances.
